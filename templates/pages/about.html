Our Story

Founded in the heart of Tebnine, Lebanon, ALJANOUB Restaurant brings authentic Lebanese flavors to your table.

Since our establishment, we've been committed to serving traditional Lebanese cuisine using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. Our dedication to quality and authenticity has made us a beloved destination for both locals and visitors.
